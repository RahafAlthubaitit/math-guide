Teaching philosophy
01
Curiosity before answers
Every lesson begins with a question. I want students to wonder before they know.
02
Family as partners
Families are the most important part of a child's education — inside and outside the classroom.
03
Technology with purpose
Digital tools — built from a CS background — make learning more personal and meaningful.
04
Growth over grades
A child who tries harder today than yesterday is already succeeding.
How I work with your child
01
Real-world mathematics
Every concept is connected to something your child can see, touch, or experience in daily life.
02
Interactive, hands-on lessons
Games, digital activities, and projects keep engagement high and make learning stick.
03
Communication in Arabic
I keep families informed at every step — in Arabic — so you always know how to support your child at home.
04
A classroom where every child belongs
Confidence matters as much as correct answers. Every student is seen, heard, and valued.
